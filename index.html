About My Life

I was born in Chicago, IL: the greatest city known to man. At least that's what people who live there tend to think. If it isn't obvious, I don't think the same. Perhaps that is because I don't still live in the city. I moved to a suburb when I was two. My parents couldn't take it! Or something like that. Actually, I probably just made that up.

Anyway, when I was a kid I wanted to be the president and change the way our government worked. Then, I wanted to be a librarian, but I didn't stick with that since everyone told me, "Briana, you aren't going to make any money!". As if they were going to be living off my salary! Then, I wanted to be a teacher and a dancer and a musician. Then there was the time I wanted to be a BIOMEDICAL ENGINEER :I know it sounds fancy, I even went to school for it at Washington University in St. Louis but, I dropped out: of the major, NOT THE SCHOOL!

Now, I don't know what I want to be. I'm trying out mechanical enginenering and perhaps a minor in computer science, but all is unclear. The only things that I am 100 percent sure I want to do in this life are (1) become a nomad. YES, A NOMAD! At least in the traveing and lying my head wherever the wind takes me sense. (2) Retire and own a ranch in Wyoming where childen can ride the horses and have fun! (3) Help people (4) Reach Nirvana. This goal would mean letting go of all my other expectations in life.
