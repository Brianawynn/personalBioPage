About My Life

I was born in Chicago, IL: the greatest city known to man. At least that's what people who live there tend to think. If it isn't obvious, I don't think the same. Perhaps that is because I don't still live in the city. I moved to a suburb when I was two. My parents couldn't take it! Or something like that. Actually, I probably just made that up.

Anyway, when I was a kid I wanted to be the president and change the way our government worked. Then I wanted to be a librarian, but everyone told me that I couldn't do that because I wouldn't make enough money. As if they were going to be living off my salary! Then I wanted to be a teacher and a dancer, and a musician. Then there was the time I wanted to be a BIOMEDICAL ENGINEER I even went to school for it at Washington University of St. Louis but, I dropped out: of the major, NOT THE SCHOOL!
